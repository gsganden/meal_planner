Skillet Chicken Parmesan With Gnocchi

        This version ditches the breading in favor of a generous dusting of flour that still creates browned and crispy edges, and calls for quick-cooking gnocchi that goes in the same pan as the chicken.         A tip about using store-bought gnocchi: While you might be tempted to purchase fresh gnocchi from the refrigerated section of the grocery store, donât. This recipe works best with the shelf-stable varieties found near the dried pasta. These are less likely to go gummy and are ideal for cooking directly in the sauce; no boiling required.
